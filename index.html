About Informatics Consultancy Firm Sierra Leone (ICF SL)

INFORMATICS CONSULTANCY FIRM SIERRA LEONE (ICF-SL), established in 2023, is a health informatics company duly registered and operating in the Republic of Sierra Leone, specializing in health information systems, data analytics, malaria surveillance, and digital health solutions, with its principal office located in Freetown, Sierra Leone.

ICF-SL possesses specialized expertise in health informatics, DHIS2 implementation, malaria epidemiology, data science, and the development of digital health solutions tailored to the needs of National Malaria Programmes and other health sector stakeholders.

Since our establishment in 2023, ICF SL has successfully delivered critical technical assistance to the National Malaria Control Programme (NMCP), including digital tools for the 2023 ITN mass campaign and the Kono school based pilot, also implemented the School Based Distribution for PMI Evolve in 2025 demonstrating proven capability and establishing a foundation of trust and collaboration. Our multidisciplinary team of statisticians, data scientists, epidemiologists, and software engineers delivers cutting edge solutions that drive evidence based decision making across Sierra Leone.

3. Proposed Methods and Data Management (RFP Part 1 Section III.C.3)

ICF Collect version 2 is a web based platform developed entirely by ICF SL using modern technologies (React, Node.js, PostgreSQL, HTML5, CSS, Java Sript and Python). It is designed for easy installation, configuration, and use, requiring no proprietary hardware or complex setup. The platform directly addresses the Scope of Work requirements.

Data Collection and Management Application Development

  • Adaptation of existing tools: We will adapt NMCP aligned forms used in 2023 to capture registration/enrollment and distribution data, incorporating feedback from PMI Evolve and NMCP.
  • Back end setup: Configuration of geography, pre loaded school enrollment data, and QR code generation for rapid data population.
  • User accounts and dashboards: Creation of all necessary user credentials and adaptation of the ICF Collect v2 dashboard to summarize key ITN indicators.
  • Server provision: Secure cloud server (DigitalOcean, PostgreSQL, Google Cloud Platform) to store all raw data throughout distribution, cleaning, and until final approval.

Training

  • Configure NMCP provided mobile devices (downloading ICF Collect app, settings) prior to trainings.
  • Provide thorough training to PMI Evolve and NMCP teams on data collection and analysis tools.
  • Update training manuals and PowerPoint presentations; support cascade training to end users.

Implementation and Post Campaign Support

  • Troubleshooting: 24/7 technical support during distribution for data collection, syncing, and analytics issues.
  • Post campaign: Data cleaning, verification, final raw data export, and dashboard finalization. Formal server spin down notification after data approval.
